About Wednesday Season 2 on Netflix

Summer of Woe: Wednesday Addams (Jenna Ortega) has returned to showcase her dark sense of humor across our screens once again. With her razor-sharp wit and trademark deadpan intact, Wednesday dives into a chilling supernatural mystery, that we will all be sure to enjoy.
