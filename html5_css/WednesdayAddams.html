Information on Upcoming Episodes

Lady Gaga makes her appearance in Wednesday as a guest role. She will be playing as Rosaline Rotwood, a mysterious teacher at Nevermore Academy, in the second part of Wednesday Season 2, which premieres on September 3, 2025. Lady Gaga was featured in a photo alongside Thing. In addition to Gaga's acting role, she will also make her first launch of her new song, "The Dead Dance," in the upcoming episodes. Lady Gaga involment was inspried by her song "Bloody Mary." If anyone can make this season even more interesting, it's definitely Lady Gaga!
