About Wednesday Addams Season 2 on Netflix

Summer of Woe: Wednesday Addams (Jenna Ortega) has returned to showcase her dark sense of humor across our screens once again. All four episodes are streaming now on Netflix, with part 2 arriving September 3rd. This season grows darker as she faces new enemies and old rivals. With her sharpened wit and trademark expressionless attitude, Wednesday plunges into a chilling supernatural mystery. Fans will also reunite with the Addams Family Morticia, Gomez, and their son Pugsley. They all step into much larger roles this season and encounter an intriguing lineup of new characters. This will be a new season for us all to enjoy!
